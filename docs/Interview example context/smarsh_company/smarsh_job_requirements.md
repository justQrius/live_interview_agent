# Smarsh Senior Solutions Engineer - Job Requirements

## Company Overview
Smarsh empowers 6,500+ organizations in regulated industries to manage risk and unleash intelligence in digital communications. Consistent leadership recognition from Gartner and Forrester. Inc. 5000 list since 2008.

## Role Summary
Primary technical point of contact for enterprise B2B sales cycle. Partner with enterprise sales, product teams, success management, and professional services. Support multiple account executives across product lines.

## Core Responsibilities

### Sales Support & Discovery
- Assist sales with discovery to identify quantifiable problems Smarsh can solve
- Demonstrate/workshop how products achieve desired business outcomes
- Provide deep technical expertise, identifying and influencing requirements early

### Technical Presentations & Solutions
- Deep technical understanding of Smarsh products and 3rd party integrations
- Conduct technical presentations and product demonstrations
- Influence and capture prospect's technical requirements
- Own architecture blueprint and revalidate with prospect

### Proof of Concept Management
- Provide technical leadership during complex, enterprise-level POCs
- Meet technical success criteria in positive, professional manner
- Exceed expectations and differentiate Smarsh from competitors

### RFI/RFP Leadership
- Take lead on technical responses for RFI/RFP/RFQ
- Prepare internal solution design proposals for complex deployments
- Work with all departments for large/complex implementations

### Knowledge Management
- Keep informed on industry news, products, services, competitors
- Stay current on legacy, existing, and emerging technologies
- Regular updates to systems of record (Salesforce, SharePoint)
- Contribute best practices and content to broader teams

### Travel
- Approximately 30-40% travel for internal/external meetings

## Key Interview Themes

### What Smarsh is Looking For

**Technical Credibility**
"Can you architect complex, multi-region deployments? Do you understand APIs, security, compliance?"

**Business Acumen**
"Can you translate technical features to business outcomes? Do you speak the language of CFOs and CCOs?"

**Sales Effectiveness**
"What's your POC win rate? How do you qualify opportunities? How do you handle objections?"

**Cultural Fit**
"Are you customer-first? Coachable? Collaborative? Outcome-driven?"

**Industry Awareness**
"Do you understand the regulatory pressures driving our customers? The competitive landscape? The market trends?"

**Innovation Mindset**
"How do you stay current? What excites you about AI/ML? Where do you see compliance technology going?"
