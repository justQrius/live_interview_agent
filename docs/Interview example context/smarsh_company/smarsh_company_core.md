# Smarsh Company Core Information

## Company Overview

**Founded**: 2001 by Stephen Marsh in Brooklyn, New York
**Headquarters**: Portland, Oregon (relocated 2004)
**Current Status**: Private Equity-backed (Vista Equity Partners & Summit Partners since March 2025)
**Latest Funding**: $250M growth round at $1.8B valuation (March 2025)
**Customer Base**: 6,500+ organizations globally including 18-19 of top 20 largest financial institutions
**Growth Rate**: 114% three-year revenue growth (2021-2024)
**Recognition**: Inc. 5000 list for 18th consecutive year (only 2 companies in history achieved this milestone)

## Mission & Vision

**Mission**: Enabling organizations to manage risk and uncover value within their electronic communications.

**Vision**: Communications data (email, chat, voice, video) represents the richest source of business intelligence within an enterprise. Smarsh transforms this unstructured data from a compliance burden into actionable intelligence.

## Core Business Pillars

Smarsh operates a continuous data lifecycle across four integrated pillars:

### 1. Capture
- Ingestion from 80-100+ communication channels
- Native format preservation (maintains threading, reactions, edits)
- Proprietary technology for difficult sources (mobile SMS, WhatsApp, WeChat, encrypted apps)
- API-based real-time ingestion (Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom)

### 2. Archive
- Cloud-native, immutable repository
- Petabyte-scale retention capability
- SEC Rule 17a-4 compliant (WORM storage)
- Separation of compute and storage for infinite scalability

### 3. Conduct (formerly Supervision)
- AI/ML-powered surveillance leveraging Digital Reasoning acquisition
- Detects financial crime (insider trading, market manipulation)
- Identifies non-financial misconduct (harassment, toxic culture)
- Context and sentiment analysis beyond keyword matching

### 4. Discovery
- Early Case Assessment (ECA) tools for legal teams
- In-house data culling and review capabilities
- Reduces reliance on expensive third-party eDiscovery vendors
- Industry-standard export formats (EDRM XML, PST, NSF)

## 2025-2026 Strategic Focus

### 1. Off-Channel Communications Crisis
**Challenge**: Regulators (SEC, CFTC) levying billions in fines for unmonitored messaging on personal devices
**Solution**: Expanding "Capture Mobile" capabilities for WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram with defensible audit trail

### 2. Cloud-Native Modernization
**Target Market**: Financial institutions with legacy on-premises systems (Veritas Enterprise Vault, EMC SourceOne)
**Strategy**: Migrate "brownfield" customers to AWS-based architecture, handling modern multimedia volumes

### 3. AI-First Compliance
**Initiative**: Deploy Generative AI and "Intelligent Agents" within surveillance workflow
**Goal**: Automate Level 1 analyst function, reduce false positives, lower compliance operational costs
**Partnership**: OpenAI integration for enhanced capture and analysis

### 4. Global Expansion
**Regions**: EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa) and APAC (Asia-Pacific)
**Drivers**: Regulatory harmonization (MiFID II), Tier 1 banking clients, data residency requirements
