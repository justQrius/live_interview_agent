## Forrester Total Economic Impact (TEI) Study

### Third-Party Validation of ROI

**Study Overview**:
- **Commissioned by**: Smarsh
- **Conducted by**: Forrester Consulting (independent research firm)
- **Methodology**: In-depth interviews with 6 global Smarsh customers
- **Purpose**: Quantify business value and ROI of Smarsh Communications Intelligence Platform

### Composite Customer Profile

**Modeled Organization**:
- **Industry**: Financial services (banking, wealth management, broker-dealer)
- **Annual Revenue**: $5 billion
- **Surveilled Employees**: 7,500 employees across multiple offices
- **Data Volume**: 625 terabytes of archived communications
- **Communication Channels**: Email, instant messaging, voice, mobile, social media

**Typical Before State**:
- Legacy on-premises archiving system (e.g., Veritas, Symantec)
- Separate point solutions for different channels (fragmented compliance view)
- Manual review processes with high false positive rates
- Limited AI/ML capabilities for risk detection
- Expensive eDiscovery via external vendors

### TEI Study Significance

**Value for SEs**:
- **Third-party credibility**: Forrester is independent, trusted by CFOs and boards
- **Peer validation**: 6 real customers (not Smarsh marketing claims)
- **Financial rigor**: Forrester applies standardized ROI methodology
- **Industry-specific**: Modeled for financial services (customer's exact vertical)

**Sales Positioning**:
"Don't just take our word for it. Forrester Consulting interviewed six of our financial services customers and modeled the ROI for a firm your size. We're happy to share the study and walk through how these outcomes apply to your specific situation."

### Key Study Components

**Financial Benefits Analyzed**:
1. **Operational Cost Savings**:
   - Reduced compliance analyst headcount (false positive reduction)
   - Consolidated vendor spend (eliminated point solutions)
   - Decommissioned legacy infrastructure (hardware, maintenance)

2. **Risk Mitigation Value**:
   - Avoided regulatory fines (SEC, FINRA)
   - Reduced legal exposure (better evidence in litigation)
   - Faster regulatory examination response

3. **Productivity Gains**:
   - Faster investigation and case closure
   - Freed analyst capacity for strategic work
   - Reduced IT support burden

4. **Revenue Enablement**:
   - Business agility (faster onboarding of new channels)
   - Competitive advantage (modern compliance infrastructure)

**Cost Factors**:
- Smarsh licensing fees
- Implementation and onboarding costs
- Change management and training
- Ongoing support and maintenance

**ROI Calculation Framework**:
- Net Present Value (NPV)
- Payback Period
- Return on Investment (ROI) percentage
- Total Economic Impact over 3-year period
